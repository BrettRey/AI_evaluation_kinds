2026-05-16 — Stop local polishing of the current zero draft. Reason: after a full read, Brett and Codex agreed the draft is clear but underwhelming; its current centre is a tidy typology rather than a sufficiently sharp philosophical claim. Future work should recast the paper around the missing projectibility norm in AI evaluation: reliability can show stability, construct validity can show what a score means in the measured setting, but neither tells us when claims may travel across models, prompts, attacks, benchmarks, or governance uses. The typology should become a diagnostic tool serving that thesis, not the thesis itself.
